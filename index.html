About Nedlia.com

Transforming Content Monetization

Nedlia.com is the industry-leading platform that bridges the gap between content creators, brands, and streaming platforms. Our cutting-edge technology enables seamless product placement integration, real-time tracking, and performance-based monetization.

We empower marketing agencies to manage multiple clients effortlessly while providing production companies with the tools they need to maximize revenue from product placements in streaming content.

Platform Overview

Explore our comprehensive suite of tools and integrations

SaaS Management Platform

Comprehensive dashboard to manage all your product placement campaigns, track budgets, monitor performance, and analyze engagement metrics in real-time.

Video Editor Plugins

Seamlessly integrate product placements directly in your editing workflow with plugins for Final Cut Pro, Adobe Premiere, DaVinci Resolve, and more. Precise timing control and metadata management built right into your favorite editing software.

Interactive Streaming Experience

Transform passive viewing into interactive shopping experiences. Smart indicators show when products are featured, and viewers can pause to explore details and make purchases.

Active Playback

Shopping cart icon appears when product placements are on screen

Paused - Product Details

Interactive overlay with product info and QR code for mobile shopping

Mobile Shopping Experience

Scan the QR code from your TV screen and seamlessly transition to mobile for instant purchasing. Works perfectly with Apple TV, Roku, and other platforms without browser access.
